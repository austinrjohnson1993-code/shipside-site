Who I am

I spent years in home services — roofing, mostly. Watched owners drop $15,000, $20,000, sometimes more on agency websites that took six seconds to load and never rang the phone once. Designers who'd never knocked a door in their life, charging for "brand discovery sessions" while the business owner's competitor two towns over was eating their lunch.

The math never made sense. The people who needed the most help online could least afford the people charging to help them. So I built a different model. No overhead. No discovery fees. No strategy theater. A clean site, built for your industry, at a price that makes sense — owned by you from day one.
