Questions before you call.

What happens after I pay?
We book a 20-minute kickoff call, collect your hours, services, photos, and contact info, then draft the site. You review it, we tighten anything that needs work, and the site goes live on day 7. Final payment lands when it launches.
Why only 7 days?
Because the offer is scoped to what a real service business needs: five pages, clear copy, your info, your photos, live fast. A short window keeps the job tight and keeps everybody from wandering off into extras.
Do I really own the site?
Yes. The site is yours. The domain is yours. The files are yours. If you stop working with Shipside, the site does not go dark.
What if I do not have a domain?
That is what The Paperwork is for. For $75 flat, we buy the domain, point it at your site, and handle the setup so you do not have to wrestle with registrars.
What's The Pocket Pager?
It is a one-time add-on that texts your phone when somebody fills out the form on your site. Name, number, what they need. You can call them back before they call the next guy.
What's The Photo Drop?
You text us a finished-job photo. We clean it up and add it to your Work gallery. It keeps the site from looking stale without giving you another system to babysit.
What if I change my hours or services?
You can update it yourself, or you can use The Oil Change and text us the update. Hours, photos, service list, crew changes - that is exactly what the monthly help is for.
Can I get help after launch?
Yes. The Oil Change is $97 per month for hosting, security, and unlimited text or photo updates. The Photo Drop is $39 per month if all you want is fresh gallery work.
I already have Facebook. Why do I need a website?
Because when somebody hears your name, they look you up. If all they find is a half-dead social page, it looks like the lights are off. A real site makes it obvious that you are open, real, and ready to call back.
What industries do you serve?
The sweet spot is blue-collar owner-operators: auto repair, roofing, HVAC, landscaping, contractors, and similar local trades. If you are not sure you fit, call anyway and we will tell you straight.
How long until my site is live?
7 days. That is the offer. Day 1 kickoff, day 2-3 build, day 4-5 review, day 7 launch.
Let me think about it.
Totally fair. We'll hold your skin choice for 48 hours. No pressure. When you're ready, call back and we'll get started.
$750 is more than I expected.
That is fair. But one service call usually covers it. One repair, one roof patch, one HVAC visit, one landscaping job. The point is to stop losing work before the call even happens.
Do I have to keep paying monthly?
No. The core site is a one-time job. Monthly help is optional. If you cancel it, the site is still yours.

Call or text. We will tell you fast if the offer fits.
