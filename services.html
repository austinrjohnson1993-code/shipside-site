What Shipside builds.

A clean website for a local service business, then a simple capture and follow-up layer so calls, quote requests, and replies don't disappear. Pick the scope on a fit call.

Who this is for

Owner-operators.

Auto repair, roofing, HVAC, landscaping, contractors, trades. One- and two-person shops and small crews.

Word-of-mouth businesses.

Most of the work comes through referrals. The site is the trust check that happens after the name gets passed along.

No site, or a stale one.

Nothing online, or something there that hasn't been touched in years.

What it solves

Word of mouth gets the name out. The phone check has to hold up - a clean place to land, the right info up front, and an easy next step. From there, calls and quote requests need somewhere to land so the busy days don't eat them.

Shipside handles the website and the capture layer in one place. One team across the build and the follow-up — no vendor handoffs between site, domain, and lead capture.

The service pieces

These are the parts inside Catch and Control. Plain names, plain jobs.

Domain Setup

We connect the site to your domain and handle the basic setup so you don't wrestle with logins.

Site Care

Hosting handled, basic checks, and quick text or photo updates when hours, services, or crew photos change.

Call Catcher

A big tap-to-call path on the site plus missed-call and follow-up text capture. When someone tries to reach you and the call gets missed, they get a simple text back so the lead doesn't drop.

Owner Inbox

Open your phone. Every missed call, every quote request, and every text reply sits in one list. We text you when something new shows up. Notes, follow-up reminders, and a monthly lead summary live in the same place. The login is owner-only and incidental — you'll mostly use the texts.

How the build runs

01
Fit call.

Fifteen minutes. We ask what you do, where you operate, how customers usually find you, and where the phone is dropping. If Shipside fits, we say so. If it doesn't, we say that too.

02
Scope and start.

We pick Launch, Catch, or Control based on what came up on the fit call. Scope, deposit, and timing land in writing before any build work starts.

03
Kickoff and draft.

Short kickoff call to collect hours, services, photos, and contact info. We draft the copy and wire up the layout around your trade.

04
Review and tighten.

You review the draft. We tighten the copy, swap anything that needs fixing, and lock the final version.

05
Launch and hand-off.

The site goes live on your domain. Files, domain access, and any capture or inbox setup are handed over. You own it.

06
Optional ongoing.

If the scope includes Site Care, Call Catcher, or Owner Inbox, we keep those running. Cancel any month - the site itself stays yours.
