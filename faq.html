01 / GETTING STARTED

Getting started

What is Shipside?
A clean owned website for a working business, wired into a capture, owner-control, and care layer behind it — so calls, quote requests, and replies stop disappearing. Four layers: L01 Owned Site, L02 Capture, L03 Owner Control, L04 Ongoing Care. Three scope tracks (foundation, capture-on, full stack) sit on top of those layers.
What's the fit call?
Fifteen minutes, no pitch deck. We ask what you do, where you operate, how customers usually find you, and where the phone is dropping. If Shipside fits, we say so and quote scope and cost in writing. If not, we tell you that on the call.
Why isn't pricing on the site?
Because the right scope depends on what's actually missing — whether the site is the gap, the call follow-up is the gap, or the owner just can't see what's coming in. We talk it through, then quote scope and cost in writing. The fit call doesn't cost anything.
What industries do you serve?
Owner-operators in working trades: auto repair, roofing, HVAC, landscaping, contractors, plumbing, electrical, welding, and similar local service businesses. One- and two-person shops and small crews. If you're not sure you fit, book a fit call anyway and we'll tell you straight.
02 / THE BUILD

The build

How fast does it go live?
Once scope is locked, usually a few days. We give ourselves up to seven so nobody gets surprised. The build stays tight: five pages, clear services, work photos, contact form, and an easy next step.
What's in Layer 02 (Capture)?
A tap-to-call path on every page plus missed-call text-back. When you miss someone, they get a simple text back asking what they need so the lead doesn't drop. Quote-request form lands in one inbox.
What's in Layer 03 (Owner Control)?
Open your phone. Every missed call, every quote request, and every text reply sits in one list. We text you when something new shows up. Notes, follow-up reminders, and a monthly lead summary live in the same place. The login is owner-only and incidental — most of the time you'll just be reading texts.
What if I don't have a domain?
We handle it. Connecting the site to your domain (or registering one if you don't have one) is part of any track that includes Layer 02 — so you don't have to wrestle with registrars.
I already have Facebook. Why do I need a website?
Because when somebody hears your name, they look you up. If all they find is a half-dead social page, it looks like the lights are off. A real owned site makes it obvious that you're open, real, and ready to call back — and a Facebook page can't run a missed-call text-back or own a domain.
03 / OWNERSHIP & OPTIONS

Ownership and options

Do I really own the site?
Yes. The files are yours. The domain is yours. The login is yours. If you stop working with Shipside, the site doesn't go dark — Layer 01 is a one-time build you own.
Do I have to keep paying monthly?
No. The site itself (Layer 01) is a one-time build you own. Layers 02–04 — capture, owner inbox, and ongoing care — only run monthly when your scope calls for them, and you can cancel any month. The site stays live either way.
Can I cancel the monthly layers?
Any month. We turn off whichever monthly layer you cancel; the site itself keeps running on your domain. No hostage retainer, no platform lock-in.
04 / AFTER LAUNCH

After launch

What if I change my hours or services?
You can update it yourself, or — if your scope includes Layer 04 (Ongoing Care) — text us the update. Hours, photos, service list, crew changes. That's what the monthly help is for.
Can I get help after launch?
Yes. Layers 02 (Capture), 03 (Owner Control), and 04 (Ongoing Care) keep running monthly if your scope includes them. Cancel any month — the site itself stays yours either way.
Let me think about it.
Totally fair. No pressure on the fit call. When you're ready to talk through scope, call or text and we'll set it up.
