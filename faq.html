Questions before you call.

What happens after I pay?
We book a 20-minute kickoff call, collect your hours, services, photos, and contact info, then draft the site. You review it, we tighten anything that needs work, and the site goes live when it is ready. Final payment lands when it launches.
How fast does it go live?
Usually in a few days. We give ourselves up to 7 so nobody gets surprised. The scope stays tight: five pages, clear services, work photos, contact form, and an easy next step.
Do I really own the site?
Yes. The site is yours. The domain is yours. The files are yours. If you stop working with Shipside, the site does not go dark.
What if I do not have a domain?
That is what Domain Setup is for. For $75, we connect the site to your domain and handle the basic setup so you do not have to wrestle with registrars.
What's Call Catcher?
It is a big tap-to-call path on the site plus missed-call and follow-up text capture. When you miss someone, they get a simple text back asking what they need.
What's Owner Inbox?
It is a private owner login for saved leads. Website requests, missed-call replies, status, notes, follow-up reminders, and a monthly lead summary live in one place.
What if I change my hours or services?
You can update it yourself, or you can use Site Care and text us the update. Hours, photos, service list, crew changes - that is exactly what the monthly help is for.
Can I get help after launch?
Yes. Site Care is $97 per month. Call Catcher is $49 per month after setup. Owner Inbox is $149 per month, with extra logins at $29 per month each.
I already have Facebook. Why do I need a website?
Because when somebody hears your name, they look you up. If all they find is a half-dead social page, it looks like the lights are off. A real site makes it obvious that you are open, real, and ready to call back.
What industries do you serve?
The sweet spot is blue-collar owner-operators: auto repair, roofing, HVAC, landscaping, contractors, and similar local trades. If you are not sure you fit, call anyway and we will tell you straight.
How long until my site is live?
Usually a few days. Up to 7 so nobody gets surprised.
Let me think about it.
Totally fair. We'll hold your design direction for 48 hours. No pressure. When you're ready, call back and we'll get started.
$750 is more than I expected.
That is fair. But one service call usually covers it. One repair, one roof patch, one HVAC visit, one landscaping job. The point is to stop losing work before the call even happens.
Do I have to keep paying monthly?
No. The core site is a one-time job. Monthly help is optional. If you cancel it, the site is still yours.
