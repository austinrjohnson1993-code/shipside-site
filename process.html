01 / THE PATH

How we work, top to bottom.

Seven steps. Each one has a clear handoff. You get a written scope before any deposit and a real owner-facing system at the end — not a dashboard you'll never log into.

Step 01

Fit call

Fifteen minutes. We ask what the business does, where leads come from, where time leaks. Straight call on whether Shipside fits — no pitch deck, no hard sell.

  • Honest call either way
  • No commitments asked of you
  • Output: yes, no, or "talk to us in three months"
Step 02

Systems map

If there's a fit, we sketch what runs the business today, where it bottlenecks, and which systems would actually move the needle. AI Workflow Review where it applies.

  • What's already wired & working
  • What's leaking time or leads
  • Output: written scope & sequence
Step 03

Foundation build

Website wedge if needed. Domain, structure, copy that fits the business. Plain files, fast, owned. Boutique higher-end builds when the scope warrants.

  • Owned files, owned domain
  • Phone-friendly, ages well
  • Holds up to a real search-and-check
Step 04

Capture & follow-up wiring

Quote Intake, Call Catcher, follow-up sequences wired in. Plain wiring where AI doesn't help, AI where it does. Tested with real numbers and real flows before handoff.

  • Call Catcher fires within seconds
  • Quote Intake routes into Owner Inbox
  • Follow-up sequences scoped to the trade
Step 05

Visibility handoff

Owner Inbox and Owner Summary turned on. We walk you through it on your phone. Push alerts, status, notes, reminders — the things you'll actually check.

  • Owner Inbox · the single owner-facing view
  • Owner Summary · weekly or monthly digest
  • Owner training in plain language
Step 06

AI & workflow expansion

Where AI earns its keep, we wire it in next. Custom Business Systems, internal tools, integrations across the stack you already pay for. Scoped to actual leverage points.

  • AI Workflow Review → concrete builds
  • Internal tools & integrations
  • Honest about where AI doesn't help
Step 07

Ongoing improvement

Hosting handled. Basic checks running. Small updates and tuning on a cadence the business sets. The site, the systems, and the files stay yours — leave any time, the build keeps running.

  • Hosting + monitoring
  • Tuning & small updates on cadence
  • You own the files · no hostage retainer
