How the comp works

This is a 1099 commission seat. There is no hourly pay and no guaranteed income. Commission isn't capped.

Qualified conversations, follow-ups, booked calls, and closed clients stack over time. The reps who run the process daily build a real book; the ones who don't, don't.

Exact commission structure is walked through on the interview so you can decide whether the math works for you before signing anything.
